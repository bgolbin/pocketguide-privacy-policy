PocketGuide Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 2, 2025

PocketGuide values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

  • Account Information: When you sign in with Google, we collect your name, email address, and profile picture for authentication purposes.
  • Location Data: We collect your precise geographic location, both in the foreground and background, to provide navigation and location-based narration features. You can disable location access in your device settings, though certain features may stop working.
  • Device Information: We collect device identifiers (such as device ID and operating system version) to maintain app security, prevent abuse, and improve performance.
  • Usage and Diagnostics: We may collect anonymous crash logs or performance data to help improve the stability of PocketGuide.

How We Use Your Information

  • To verify your identity and sign you into the app.
  • To provide location-based narration and route guidance.
  • To personalize your in-app experience and improve app performance.
  • To maintain security and prevent unauthorized access.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal information. Data is shared only as necessary:

  • With trusted service providers that help us operate PocketGuide (e.g., cloud hosting, analytics, authentication).
  • When required by law or to protect the rights and safety of users or others.

Data Retention

We retain your account and device information for as long as your PocketGuide account remains active. Location data is stored only temporarily and is not retained once your trip session ends, unless you explicitly save it. You may request data deletion at any time.

Your Choices and Rights

  • You can revoke PocketGuide’s access to your Google account at any time via your Google Account settings.
  • You can disable location tracking through your device settings. Some features will no longer function.
  • You can request deletion of your data by contacting us at bgolbin@gmail.com.

Children’s Privacy

PocketGuide is not directed toward children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If we learn that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. If we make significant changes, we will notify users within the app or via email. The “Effective Date” above indicates the most recent update.
